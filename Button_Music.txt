Ini adalah daftar perintah Shinobi musik :

⚙️ Perintah untuk semua user :
▪️/play - reply to youtube url or song file to play song
▪️/vplay - reply to youtube url or song file to play song
▪️/song <song name> - download songs you want quickly
▪️/search <query> - search videos on youtube with details
▪️/lyrics <query> - search lyrics from web

⚙️ Perintah yang membutuhkan hak Admin :
▪️/pause - pause song play
▪️/resume - resume song play
▪️/skip - play next song
▪️/end - stop music play
▪️/vstop - stop video stream
▪️/vpause - pause video stream
▪️/vresume - resume video stream
▪️/vskip - skip video stream

⚙️ Perintah yang membutuhkan hak Sudo :
▪️/userbotleaveall - remove assistant from all chats
▪️/broadcast <reply to message> - globally brodcast replied message to all chats
▪️/pmpermit [on/off] - enable/disable pmpermit message
